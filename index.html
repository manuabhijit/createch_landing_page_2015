CREATECH

TECHNICAL CLUB OF JUIT

Createch organizes the annual technical fest of JUIT- ‘MURIOUS’ which helps in bringing out the hidden talent in the budding minds. Murious, now in its tenth phase, was started in 2006. Murious has gone far ahead than being just a technical fest and series of mind-blowing events. It provides JUITians an environment different from their daily routine by organizing events having a blend of technology and fun like robotics, LAN gaming, paintball etc. Its goal is to portray the ideas of the students in order to enhance their technical and creative skills.
